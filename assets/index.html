About Me

Hello there! I'm Mario Daniel Panuco, though I go by Daniel. Having recently graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at UCSC, I am now starting my journey this fall as a Masters student in Scientific Computing and Applied Mathematics at UCSC. My undergraduate years sparked a passion in me for Scientific Computing, Artificial Intelligence, and Systems Design, a passion I plan to further cultivate throughout my Masters program.

I am particularly drawn to research in dynamical systems for autonomous vehicles and more broadly complex systems that benefit from the integration of artificial intelligence, computer vision, and control optimization.

Outside of academia, my interests are in digital/analog sound design (it's all sines and cosines!!).

This personal website serves as a platform for me to explore and construct a monolith Rust server, allowing me to focus on back-end complexities while maintaining a simple and mostly static front-end.
